Simons Observatory UK

The Simons Observatory is an international project, led by the US, supported by the Simons Foundation and the Heising-Simons Foundation, and includes 85 institutes from 13 countries.

From October 2022, 6 universities are delivering the major new UK contribution - Cambridge, Cardiff, Imperial College London, Manchester, Oxford and Sussex.
