e-MERLIN Time Awards

e-MERLIN is operated by the University of Manchester as a National Facility of the Science & Technology Facilities Council (STFC), and is available for use on a peer-review basis, with observing proposals dealt with by the Panel for Allocation of Telescope Time (PATT). The links below give details of observing time allocated during each of the observing semesters since 2012.

Cycle 1 Time Awards

In addition to observing time allocated via the Panel for Allocation of Telescope Time (PATT) a series of large e-MERLIN Legacy projects have been allocated on a peer-review basis to several consortia. Observations for the e-MERLIN legacy programme run in parallel to PATT allocated observations. A full list of these legacy projects along with links to submitted proposals is available from the e-MERLIN legacy project pages.
